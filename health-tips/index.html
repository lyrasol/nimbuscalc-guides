Health & Wellness Insights

Beyond BMI and calories, several other metrics can give you a fuller picture of your health. This page covers body fat percentage, metabolic age, and pregnancy health indicators.

Body Fat Percentage

Body fat percentage is a better indicator of fitness than weight alone. Healthy ranges vary by age and gender. The U.S. Navy method (using circumference measurements) is a simple way to estimate it.

Use our body fat calculator to get your estimate in under a minute.

Healthy body fat ranges (adults):
Women: 21–32% (age 20–39), 23–33% (40–59), 24–35% (60–79)
Men: 8–19% (20–39), 11–21% (40–59), 13–24% (60–79)

Metabolic Age

Metabolic age compares your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) to the average for your chronological age. If your metabolic age is lower, it means your metabolism is healthier than average for your age group.

Check your BMR with our BMR calculator and see how you compare.
